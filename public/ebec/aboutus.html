About Us

BEST İzmir, one of the local groups, has been operating at Ege University since 2008. In addition to developing students and preparing them for life after graduation by carrying out activities in areas such as engineering competitions and academic courses; With its fun and friendly side, it enables its members to be together in a friendly and cheerful environment.
